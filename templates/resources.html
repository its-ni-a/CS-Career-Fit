Resources & Tools For Those Interested In Computer Science

Tools, Tech & News for a Career in Computer Science

The continued explosion of applications developed for mobile, combined with near constant technological innovation across the board, has powered the call for more computer science degree holders, researchers, and technicians. Keeping up-to-date and educated on developments and news in the computer science industry isn’t just a bonus anymore – it’s a must. Here, find links to organizations and associations supporting the education and career advancement of professionals, including job sites, professional journals, continued learning opportunities and career networking.



Popular Websites Used By Professionals

Check out some helpful wesbites in the Computer science field!

Bootstrap is a CSS framework that helps users create beautiful websites with ease and accessibility. Their site has explanations of how to use Bootstrap, and it can be learnt in just a few days - we even used components of Bootstrap to make this website!

W3Schools has explanations of different coding problems and abilities! If you have an issue while coding or are curious as to how to do something, a simple Google search with "W3Schools" on the end will give you an article to solve your problem. It's a great resource for debugging and learning new methods!
Google's Cloudshell is a simple service that allows you to code with multiple types (Python, Java, etc) all in your web browser. It also allows you to open multiple projects and work on them at once, and you can even check your work via Google preview-hosting your sites and apps!
